I'm Naveen KP — a BTech Computer Science engineer from Calicut University with 9 years of professional frontend experience across startups, mid-cap IT companies and global MNCs like Accenture and Altimetrik.

Then life took a beautiful turn. I travelled Himachal Pradesh, got married to a Yoga trainer, and discovered that visual storytelling and creative design lit me up in ways code never fully did. I began creating content for wellness brands — designing, shooting, editing — and couldn't stop.

Today I build using a vibe-first, AI-assisted approach — where intuition, tools like Cursor and Lovable, and rapid iteration replace conventional line-by-line coding. I sit at the intersection of technical depth, creative instinct and AI leverage, and I am Co-Founder & Creative Head of brahmaspace.com.
